Research Focus

My research focuses on enabling robots, particularly social robots, to better understand and interact with their environments through the integration of spatially-grounded semantic information. By combining symbolic knowledge with spatial awareness, I aim to create systems that allow robots to interpret, store, and utilize spatially-grounded data seamlessly, enhancing their ability to collaborate in complex (social) settings.
